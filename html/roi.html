Resale Strategy

Target Buyer Personas

  • Webflow / web developers managing 3+ client sites
  • Design agencies doing website redesigns
  • Freelancers who send .loom videos for feedback now
  • Marketing agencies managing landing page campaigns
  • SaaS founders getting QA feedback on staging
  • No-code builders delivering Webflow/Framer projects

Sales Channels

  • BOOM B.A.R dashboard (cross-sell to existing users)
  • ProductHunt launch (free exposure, early adopters)
  • Webflow community forums + Facebook groups
  • Agency Slack communities (e.g. Agency Hive)
  • YouTube / TikTok demo video ("how I get client feedback")
  • Appsumo lifetime deal (rapid user acquisition)

Early Access Hook

  • Code APPROVE50 = 50% off for 12 months
  • First 50 spots only — creates urgency
  • Lock in early pricing forever
  • Beta users = product feedback + testimonials
  • Turn beta users into affiliates (20% recurring)

Expansion Revenue

  • Free → Pro conversion when 1-project limit hit
  • Pro → Agency when team grows past 3 members
  • Yearly billing upsell (2 months free)
  • White-label add-on for agency resellers
  • Future: AI-powered feedback summary ($5 add-on)
